You can use this service if the NHS COVID-19 app has detected that you have been in contact with a person who has coronavirus. This means you are advised to self-isolate.

If you are on a low income, are unable to work from home and will lose income as a result of self-isolating, you may be entitled to a payment of £500 from your local authority under the Test and Trace Support Payment scheme.

You will need to answer some questions in the next section to check whether you could be eligible.

Before you start

You cannot claim this payment more than once within a self-isolation period. You can claim it multiple times for separate self-isolation periods, as long as they do not overlap with each other.

As part of the application process, you will register with NHS Test and Trace. To claim this payment, you need to register before your self-isolation period finishes.

When you register with NHS Test and Trace, you will be legally required to self-isolate. If you do not comply, you may face a penalty of at least £1,000.

If you have technical problems while you are using this service, select the ‘Financial support’ button in the app and find the link for Help to apply for the Test and Trace Support Payment.
