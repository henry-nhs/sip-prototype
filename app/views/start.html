This service is to help people that have been notified to self-isolate through the NHS Covid-19 app.

Privacy

By using this service, your privacy is not affected:

  • the app still does not know who you are
  • the app cannot track you
  • your data is not fed back into the app

Legal

If you successfully claim a support payment:

  • you are legally obliged to self-isolate
  • you cannot claim another payment in the same period

Eligibility

You may be eligible for a payment, if you:

  • have been told to self-isolate through the app, through close contact with somebody that has tested positive
  • are employed / self-employed and unable to work from home and suffering a loss of income
  • are currently claiming at least one of these benefits
  • or are on a low income
