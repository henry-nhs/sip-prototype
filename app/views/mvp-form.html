Important information

When you register with NHS Test and Trace, you will be legally required to self-isolate. You must not leave home for any reason other than a genuine emergency – for example, to seek medical care or to escape harm. If you do not comply, you may face a penalty of at least £1,000 or prosecution and a fine.

You are advised:

  • not to have visitors in your home, including friends and family – except for essential care
  • to try to avoid contact with anyone you live with as much as possible
  • to order food or medicine online or by phone, or ask friends and family to drop it off at your home

If you do not have symptoms:

  • people you live with do not need to self-isolate
  • people in your support bubble do not need to self-isolate

The app still cannot be used to track you.
