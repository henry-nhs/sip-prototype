Important information

When you submit your details to NHS Test and Trace, you will be legally required to self-isolate. This means you must:

  • not leave your home for any reason – if you need food or medicine, order it online or by phone, or ask friends and family to drop it off at your home
  • not have visitors in your home, including friends and family – except for essential care
  • try to avoid contact with anyone you live with as much as possible

If you do not have symptoms:

  • people you live with do not need to self-isolate
  • people in your support bubble do not need to self-isolate

If you do not comply, you may face a penalty of at least £1,000.

The app still cannot be used to track you.
