To apply for this payment, you will need to complete the following steps.

1. {{step1Label}}

Log in or register for an NHS account so we can check who you are.

2. {{step2Label}}

You will be asked to enter your name and postcode, so that NHS Test and Trace can check for duplicate applications.

3. {{step3Label}}

You will receive an email and text message from NHS Test and Trace (this usually takes around 30 minutes).

You will need to enter some personal details to register for NHS Test and Trace if you haven’t already done this.

When you’ve registered, you’ll receive an account number (sometimes known as a CTAS number).

4. {{step4Label}}

You’ll receive a link to your local authority’s website.

Your local authority will ask you for your NHS Test and Trace ID - the 8-digit number you receive when you have registered with NHS Test and Trace. Your local authority may call this something slightly different, such as an account number or CTAS number.

They will also ask you for at least:

  • proof of your identity
  • proof of benefits you’ve received
  • your employment details (including National Insurance number)
  • your bank account details

5. {{step5Label}}

If your application is successful, you will receive the Test and Trace Support Payment from your local authority.
